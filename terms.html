1. The service

Program name: CallCaught.

Program description: CallCaught is an automated missed-call text-back service operated on behalf of participating local businesses. When a caller dials a participating business and the call is not answered, CallCaught sends the caller a single automated SMS text message on behalf of that business, letting them know their call was received and inviting them to reply. Any reply is relayed to the business so it can respond. CallCaught does not send marketing, promotional, or recurring messages.

2. Consent, call to action, and message frequency

Call to action / opt-in: The sole way to receive a message from CallCaught is to place a phone call to a participating business's phone number. By placing a phone call to a participating business's phone number, you consent to receive an automated text message in direct response to that call. This call-to-action is disclosed on this page and on the CallCaught website (callcaught.co) in the section titled "For callers — how the text-back works." Participating businesses may also disclose it on their own websites and voicemail greetings.

Message frequency is low — typically one message per missed call, plus any conversational replies you choose to engage in. You will not receive messages unrelated to a call you placed.

4. Opt-out and help

You can cancel the SMS service at any time. Text STOP in reply to any message to opt out. After you send STOP, you will receive one final message confirming you have been unsubscribed, and no further messages will be sent to you through CallCaught on behalf of that business unless you opt back in.

If you are experiencing issues with the messaging program or need assistance, reply with the keyword HELP, or contact support directly at jawjags@outlook.com.
